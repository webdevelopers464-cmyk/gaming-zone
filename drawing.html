🎨 How to Play:

Welcome to the Drawing Challenge! You will be given a shape to draw in each level.

Use your mouse (or finger on touch screen) to draw on the canvas.

Each level has a different shape to draw: Circle, Square, Triangle, and more.

You'll get helpful feedback. Try your best!
